Refund Policy

This Refund Policy explains when returns are accepted, how approved returns must be sent back, and how refunds are processed.

1. Return Window

Return requests for eligible items must be submitted within 10 days of delivery.

2. Approval Required

All returns must be approved before the item is sent back. Returns sent without approval may be rejected.

3. Return Conditions

  • Items must be unused and returned in perfect original condition.
  • All original tags, packaging, boxes, dustbags, and accessories must be included.
  • Items showing wear, scent, washing, damage, or other signs of use may be rejected.

4. Return Shipping Costs

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective, damaged upon arrival, or incorrectly shipped.

5. Approved Carriers

Returns should be sent with active tracking using carriers such as UPS, DHL, or FedEx. Returns sent through standard postal services may be rejected.

6. Defective or Incorrect Items

If an item arrives damaged, defective, or incorrect, notify us within 5 days of delivery and include clear photos. Claims are subject to review and verification by the fulfillment partner.

7. Customs and Additional Charges

Any customs duties, clearance fees, or related charges associated with a return are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to our error or a verified fulfillment issue.

8. Refund Timing

Approved refunds are issued after the returned item is received, inspected, and approved.

9. Contact

For return or refund questions, contact corporate@prestigeapparelgroup.com.

Prestige Apparel Group is operated by RGC4, a U.S.-based company.
