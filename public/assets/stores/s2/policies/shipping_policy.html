Shipping Policy

This shipping policy explains how orders are processed, shipped, and delivered.

1. Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1 to 3 business days after payment is confirmed.

2. Estimated Delivery

Most orders arrive within 5 to 10 business days after shipment, depending on destination, carrier performance, customs processing, and supplier location.

3. Shipping Origins

Orders may ship from domestic or international fulfillment locations and may arrive in supplier packaging.

4. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are shown at checkout unless a promotion states otherwise.

5. Delays

Delivery delays may occur due to weather, customs, carrier issues, high-volume periods, or events outside our reasonable control.

6. Address Accuracy

Customers are responsible for providing a complete and accurate shipping address. If an order is returned because of incorrect or incomplete address information, the customer is responsible for any additional shipping, return, handling, or customs charges.

7. Unclaimed Packages

If a package is unclaimed and returned, the customer is responsible for the costs associated with the return and any reshipment.

8. Lost Packages

If a package is confirmed lost in transit, please contact us promptly so we can open a claim with the carrier or fulfillment partner.

9. Contact

For shipping questions, use the Contact Us page.
