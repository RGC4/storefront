Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how it is shared when you use our website or make a purchase.

1. Who We Are

Prestige Apparel Group is an online retail store offering premium products.

2. Information We Collect

When you place an order or use our website, we may collect information such as your name, shipping and billing address, email address, phone number, payment details, IP address, browser type, device information, cookies, and usage data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process and deliver orders, communicate with you, provide customer support, improve our website and customer experience, prevent fraud and unauthorized transactions, and send marketing communications that you may opt out of at any time.

4. How Your Information Is Shared

We share your information only as necessary to operate the store, including with payment processors, shipping carriers, trusted third-party suppliers who fulfill and ship orders, and service providers that support store operations.

Our store is hosted on Shopify, which provides the ecommerce platform used to process orders and transactions.

We do not sell your personal information.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep the site functioning properly, understand how visitors use the site, and support marketing and advertising. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary to complete transactions, provide support, comply with legal obligations, and operate our business.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information and may opt out of marketing communications at any time.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information. No online system is completely secure.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

10. Updates to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions, use the Contact Us page.
